Our Story

A legacy of hospitality and excellence in Gondar since 1985

Our History

From humble beginnings to Gondar's premier hospitality destination

Ethiopia Hotel was founded in 1985 by Alemayehu Kebede, a visionary entrepreneur who recognized the need for quality accommodation in Gondar. What started as a modest 12-room guesthouse has grown into a 50-room premier hotel, serving travelers from around the world.

Over the decades, we have preserved our commitment to authentic Ethiopian hospitality while embracing modern amenities. Our hotel has witnessed Gondar's transformation and has played a vital role in welcoming tourists to Ethiopia's historical treasures.

Today, Ethiopia Hotel stands as a landmark of comfort and reliability in Gondar. We are proud to have served generations of travelers and continue our tradition of excellence for many years to come.

Our Journey

Milestones in our history of hospitality

1985

Hotel Founding

Ethiopia Hotel opens with 12 rooms, becoming one of Gondar's first modern guesthouses

1995

First Expansion

Hotel expands to 25 rooms and adds restaurant service, introducing traditional Ethiopian cuisine

2005

Major Renovation

Complete renovation with modern amenities while preserving traditional Ethiopian architectural elements

2015

30th Anniversary

Celebrating 30 years of service with expansion to 50 rooms and new conference facilities

2023

Digital Transformation

Launch of modern website and digital services to better serve international travelers
