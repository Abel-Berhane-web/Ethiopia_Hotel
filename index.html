Why Choose Ethiopia Hotel

Discover what makes us the preferred choice for travelers in Gondar

Prime Location

Centrally located in Gondar, just minutes from Fasil Ghebbi, Royal Enclosure, and other UNESCO World Heritage sites. Perfect for exploring the city's rich history.

Authentic Hospitality

Experience genuine Ethiopian hospitality with a legacy of serving guests for decades. Our staff is dedicated to making your stay memorable and comfortable.

Exquisite Dining

Savor traditional Ethiopian cuisine and international dishes at our restaurant. Fresh ingredients, authentic flavors, and warm ambiance await you.

A Legacy of Hospitality Since 1985

Ethiopia Hotel has been a landmark of comfort and hospitality in Gondar for over three decades. Founded with a vision to provide travelers with authentic Ethiopian warmth combined with modern amenities, we have hosted guests from around the world.

Our hotel combines traditional Ethiopian architecture with contemporary design, creating a unique atmosphere that reflects both our heritage and commitment to modern comfort. Each space is thoughtfully designed to ensure your stay is both memorable and relaxing.
